Free Annotation, Universal Across Modes

Going beyond traditional collages, we provide powerful canvas tools that are **fully supported in both layout and stitching modes**, letting your images speak.

  • Canvas Text Tool
    Add text anywhere on the canvas. Easily change color and font size with the floating toolbar, and double-click to edit content directly.
  • Canvas Arrow Tool
    Add fully controllable vector arrows. Drag the start, end, and rotation handles to freely adjust their shape, and instantly change color and thickness.
  • (New!) Boxes & Circles
    Easily highlight key areas or use ovals for creative compositions. All shapes support free dragging, 8-point scaling, and style customization.

Layout Mode: Pro-Level Control

Start with a huge selection of templates and create unique grid collages with intuitive drag, pan, and zoom controls.

  • Massive Layout Templates
    Dozens of professional layouts for 2 to 16 images to suit any scenario.
  • Real-time Drag & Adjust
    Drag dividers to customize cell sizes; drag images to swap positions; hold Alt to pan images for precise composition.
  • (New!) Image Zoom & Pan
    Zoom with the mouse wheel, two fingers, or dedicated buttons, and use arrow keys for pixel-perfect positioning.

Stitching Mode: Endless Creativity

Easily stitch multiple images into one long strip. Whether it's chat logs, web captures, or movie subtitles, you can now add rich annotations after stitching.

  • Horizontal & Vertical Support
    Switch between horizontal or vertical stitching with one click to meet different needs.
  • Drag & Drop Sorting
    Directly drag images on the canvas to easily reorder them for a more intuitive and efficient workflow.
  • (New!) Creative Tools Supported
    You can now freely add text, arrows, boxes, and all other annotations to your final stitched image!
